Characters

Characters are obtainable units in Genshin Impact. They come in different rarities (4-Star and 5-Star), aligned with an Element, equipable with a specific Weapon, and belong to a Region.

Image taken from: Genshin Impact Fandom

Character rarities ▾

In the world of Teywat, there are two tiers of playable characters, yes, you guessed right, 4-star and 5-star characters. There is a major difference in the power each tier holds. While most 5-star characters are only attainable on Limited Event Wishes, the 4-star characters can be found on every wish banner. As a result the constellations of the lower tier characters are easier attained.

Elements ▾

Anemo, Geo, Electro, Dendro, Hydro, Pyro, Cyro - these are the different elemental energies that can be found in the world of Teyvat. Each element can interact with one another producing an elemental reaction depending on the type of element used. Of the different elements there are some which, when applied to an enemy, has a specific duration before it disappears - this excludes Geo and Anemo - which is the perfect interval to trigger an elemental reaction! Weapons ▾

Every respective character in the world of Teyvat has a specific weapon affinity. Sword, Claymore, Catalyst, Polearm and Bow users are very prominent. These playable characters share different playstyles that are uncustomizable, one of these playstyles might be suit for you and knows there might be new weapon users introduced in the near future!

Regions ▾

The Capital of Teyvat? No such thing! Teyvat is split into different regions which are governed and conform to the customs of their god and Archon. Currently only four regions have been made explorable by Hoyoverse which is Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, Sumeru - however, Hoyoverse is sure to release the other regions, like Fontaine in future version updates.
